Centuries of Naval Excellence

1624

Birth of the Royal Navy

Cardinal Richelieu establishes the foundation of French naval power under Louis XIII, creating the first organized French fleet.

1805

Battle of Trafalgar

Though defeated, the French Navy demonstrated remarkable courage against the British fleet, shaping future naval doctrine and honor.

1940-1944

World War II Resistance

The Free French Naval Forces played a crucial role in liberation operations and maintained French maritime presence worldwide.

1960-Present

Nuclear Age & Modern Fleet

France becomes a nuclear naval power with advanced submarines and the Charles de Gaulle aircraft carrier, maintaining global maritime influence.
